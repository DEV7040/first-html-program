A. Agriculture & Farmer Welfare Schemes in Maharashtra (2025)

Schemes include eligibility, core benefits, required documents, and official portals.

MaharashtraFarmers

1) Krishi Samruddhi Yojana

Eligibility
Small & marginal farmers, SC/ST, women farmers, PwDs, FPOs.
Benefits
Support for micro‑irrigation, climate‑resilient farming, mechanization, crop diversification.
Documents
Agristac ID, Aadhaar, income certificate, land records, bank passbook.
mahasdb.maharashtra.gov.in
MaharashtraDrought‑prone

2) Nanaji Deshmukh Krishi Sanjivani Yojana

Eligibility
Farmers in drought‑prone areas.
Benefits
Soil improvement, drought resilience support.
Documents
Aadhaar, land documents, domicile proof.
agri.maharashtra.gov.in
SC/Neo‑BuddhistIrrigation

3) Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Krishi Swavalamban Yojana

Eligibility
SC/Neo‑Buddhist farmers with wells/ponds.
Benefits
Subsidy for wells, ponds, irrigation pumps, micro‑irrigation (up to 90%).
Documents
Caste certificate, land/water asset proof, Aadhaar.
nabard.org
Small/Marginal0.6+ ha

4) Magel Tyala Shet Tale Yojana (Farm Pond)

Eligibility
Small/marginal farmers with ≥ 0.6 ha land.
Benefits
₹50,000 subsidy for farm pond construction.
Documents
Land record, Aadhaar, income certificate.
agri.maharashtra.gov.in
SolarIrrigation

5) Mukhyamantri Saur Krushi Pump Yojana

Eligibility
All registered farmers in Maharashtra.
Benefits
Subsidized solar pumps for irrigation.
Documents
Aadhaar, land documents, bank passbook.
myscheme.gov.in

Eligibility
All landholding farmers.
Benefits
₹6,000/year in 3 DBT installments.
Documents
Aadhaar, land proof, bank passbook.
pmkisan.gov.in

Eligibility
All PM‑Kisan beneficiaries.
Benefits
Additional state DBT top‑up.
Documents
Aadhaar, PM‑Kisan ID, voter ID.
mahaagri.gov.in
Insurance

8) PM Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Eligibility
All crop growers (voluntary/loanee).
Benefits
Crop insurance for natural calamities.
Documents
Aadhaar, land/crop proof.
pmfby.gov.in
Storage

Eligibility
Builders or farmers constructing warehouses.

Capital subsidy for rural storage.

Project report, Aadhaar, land approval.

Group farming

Eligibility
Groups of 10+ farmers with 100+ acres combined.

B. Government Educational Schemes in Maharashtra

1) Post Matric Scholarship (SC/ST/OBC/SBC/VJNT)

Eligibility
Students from the listed categories studying post‑matric courses.
Benefits
Tuition & exam fees, maintenance allowance.
Documents
Caste & income certificates, mark sheet, Aadhaar, bank passbook.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

2) Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Scholarship

Eligibility
SC students with ≥ 75% in 10th.
Benefits
100% tuition & exam fee waiver.
Documents
10th marksheet, caste & income certificates, Aadhaar.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

3) Eklavya Scholarship

Eligibility
Arts/Commerce grads with ≥ 60%, Science with ≥ 70%.
Benefits
₹5,000 annually.
Documents
Degree marksheet, income certificate, Aadhaar, bank passbook.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

4) Merit Scholarship for Economically Backward Class (EBC)

Eligibility
Students from EBC with high academic merit.
Benefits
Cash incentive based on merit.
Documents
Income certificate, previous year marksheet, Aadhaar, bank details.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

5) State Government Open Merit Scholarship

Eligibility
SSC ≥ 60% and pursuing higher secondary.
Benefits
₹100 per month for two years.
Documents
SSC marksheet, Aadhaar, income certificate, school certificate.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

C. Social Welfare Schemes (SC/ST, OBCs, Minorities, Differently Abled)

1) Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Anudan Yojana

Eligibility
Age 18–65; SC/ST, OBCs, Differently‑abled, destitute; BPL; no income.
Benefits
₹600/month (individual), ₹900/month (2+ beneficiaries).
Documents
Aadhaar, BPL card, Income & Disability certificate (if applicable), age proof, bank passbook.
sjsa.maharashtra.gov.in

2) Shravan Bal Seva Pension Scheme

Eligibility
Senior citizens (65+) BPL; not receiving central pension.
Benefits
₹600/month pension.
Documents
Aadhaar, BPL ration card, age & domicile, bank passbook.
sjsa.maharashtra.gov.in

3) Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension

Eligibility
Widows aged 40–59 from BPL households.
Benefits
₹300–₹500/month (state‑wise).
Documents
Aadhaar, death certificate, income certificate, BPL proof, bank passbook.
nsap.nic.in

4) Indira Gandhi Disability Pension Scheme

Eligibility
Persons 18–59 with ≥80% disability from BPL households.
Benefits
₹300–₹500/month (state‑wise).
Documents
Aadhaar, disability certificate, income certificate, BPL proof, bank passbook.
nsap.nic.in

5) Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya Yojana

Eligibility
Residents of Maharashtra with Yellow/Orange ration card; SC/ST or EWS.
Benefits
Free treatment up to ₹1.5 lakh/year in empaneled hospitals.
Documents
Aadhaar, ration card, caste certificate (if applicable), referral/diagnosis from hospital.
jeevandayee.gov.in

D. Energy & Environment Schemes

1) Solar Pump Subsidy Scheme (PM‑KUSUM)

Eligibility
Farmers (small, marginal, individual or groups); cultivable land & water source or willing to lease land for solar infrastructure.
Benefits
60% subsidy, 30% loan, 10% farmer contribution for solar pump installation.
Documents
Aadhaar, land ownership proof, bank passbook, passport photo.
mnre.gov.in

2) Unnat Jyoti by Affordable LEDs (UJALA)

Eligibility
All households & public institutions with valid electricity connection.
Benefits
LED bulbs & appliances at subsidized prices.
Documents
Aadhaar, address proof, electricity bill.
eeslindia.org

3) Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

Eligibility
Women from BPL families without an existing LPG connection.
Benefits
Free LPG connection, first refill, and stove under central support.
Documents
Aadhaar, BPL card, ration card, age & ID proof.
pmuy.gov.in

4) Jal Shakti Abhiyan / Water Schemes

Eligibility
Rural communities in water‑stressed regions.
Benefits
Groundwater recharge, rainwater harvesting, water body restoration & watershed development.
Documents
Aadhaar, project proposals via NGOs/Gram Panchayats, local authority approval.
wr.gov.in
