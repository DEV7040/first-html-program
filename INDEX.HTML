A. Agriculture & Farmer Welfare Schemes in Maharashtra (2025)

Schemes include eligibility, core benefits, required documents, and official portals.

B. Government Educational Schemes in Maharashtra

1) Post Matric Scholarship (SC/ST/OBC/SBC/VJNT)

Eligibility
Students from the listed categories studying post‑matric courses.
Benefits
Tuition & exam fees, maintenance allowance.
Documents
Caste & income certificates, mark sheet, Aadhaar, bank passbook.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

2) Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Scholarship

Eligibility
SC students with ≥ 75% in 10th.
Benefits
100% tuition & exam fee waiver.
Documents
10th marksheet, caste & income certificates, Aadhaar.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

3) Eklavya Scholarship

Eligibility
Arts/Commerce grads with ≥ 60%, Science with ≥ 70%.
Benefits
₹5,000 annually.
Documents
Degree marksheet, income certificate, Aadhaar, bank passbook.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

4) Merit Scholarship for Economically Backward Class (EBC)

Eligibility
Students from EBC with high academic merit.
Benefits
Cash incentive based on merit.
Documents
Income certificate, previous year marksheet, Aadhaar, bank details.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

5) State Government Open Merit Scholarship

Eligibility
SSC ≥ 60% and pursuing higher secondary.
Benefits
₹100 per month for two years.
Documents
SSC marksheet, Aadhaar, income certificate, school certificate.
mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in

C. Social Welfare Schemes (SC/ST, OBCs, Minorities, Differently Abled)

1) Sanjay Gandhi Niradhar Anudan Yojana

Eligibility
Age 18–65; SC/ST, OBCs, Differently‑abled, destitute; BPL; no income.
Benefits
₹600/month (individual), ₹900/month (2+ beneficiaries).
Documents
Aadhaar, BPL card, Income & Disability certificate (if applicable), age proof, bank passbook.
sjsa.maharashtra.gov.in

2) Shravan Bal Seva Pension Scheme

Eligibility
Senior citizens (65+) BPL; not receiving central pension.
Benefits
₹600/month pension.
Documents
Aadhaar, BPL ration card, age & domicile, bank passbook.
sjsa.maharashtra.gov.in

3) Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension

Eligibility
Widows aged 40–59 from BPL households.
Benefits
₹300–₹500/month (state‑wise).
Documents
Aadhaar, death certificate, income certificate, BPL proof, bank passbook.
nsap.nic.in

4) Indira Gandhi Disability Pension Scheme

Eligibility
Persons 18–59 with ≥80% disability from BPL households.
Benefits
₹300–₹500/month (state‑wise).
Documents
Aadhaar, disability certificate, income certificate, BPL proof, bank passbook.
nsap.nic.in

5) Mahatma Phule Jan Arogya Yojana

Eligibility
Residents of Maharashtra with Yellow/Orange ration card; SC/ST or EWS.
Benefits
Free treatment up to ₹1.5 lakh/year in empaneled hospitals.
Documents
Aadhaar, ration card, caste certificate (if applicable), referral/diagnosis from hospital.
jeevandayee.gov.in

D. Energy & Environment Schemes

1) Solar Pump Subsidy Scheme (PM‑KUSUM)

Eligibility
Farmers (small, marginal, individual or groups); cultivable land & water source or willing to lease land for solar infrastructure.
Benefits
60% subsidy, 30% loan, 10% farmer contribution for solar pump installation.
Documents
Aadhaar, land ownership proof, bank passbook, passport photo.
mnre.gov.in

2) Unnat Jyoti by Affordable LEDs (UJALA)

Eligibility
All households & public institutions with valid electricity connection.
Benefits
LED bulbs & appliances at subsidized prices.
Documents
Aadhaar, address proof, electricity bill.
eeslindia.org

3) Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

Eligibility
Women from BPL families without an existing LPG connection.
Benefits
Free LPG connection, first refill, and stove under central support.
Documents
Aadhaar, BPL card, ration card, age & ID proof.
pmuy.gov.in

4) Jal Shakti Abhiyan / Water Schemes

Eligibility
Rural communities in water‑stressed regions.
Benefits
Groundwater recharge, rainwater harvesting, water body restoration & watershed development.
Documents
Aadhaar, project proposals via NGOs/Gram Panchayats, local authority approval.
wr.gov.in

E. Health & Nutrition Schemes

1) Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY)

Eligibility
BPL families, SC/ST, farmers, senior citizens; Yellow/Orange ration card in Maharashtra.
Benefits
Free treatment up to ₹1.5–2 lakh/year at empaneled hospitals.
Documents
Aadhaar, ration card, hospital referral, income certificate.
jeevandayee.gov.in

2) Ayushman Bharat Yojana (PM‑JAY)

Eligibility
BPL families across India based on SECC data; name should appear in SECC 2011.
Benefits
Health insurance cover of ₹5 lakh/year/family for secondary & tertiary hospitalization.
Documents
Aadhaar, ration card, PM‑JAY card (if available), SECC ID.
pmjay.gov.in

3) Bal Sanjeevani (Child Nutrition)

Eligibility
Malnourished children under 6 years identified by Anganwadi/PHC.
Benefits
Free nutritional kits, regular health checkups, supplements, referrals.
Documents
Child health card, Anganwadi/PHC certificate, Aadhaar (if available).

4) Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)

Eligibility
Pregnant women (esp. rural SC/ST, BPL).
Benefits
₹700–₹1,400 financial aid for institutional delivery & transport.
Documents
Aadhaar, delivery record, BPL card, Mother & Child Health card.
nhm.gov.in

5) TB Control, HIV/AIDS & Disability Care

Eligibility
TB patients, HIV+ individuals, Persons with Disabilities (as per govt diagnosis); BPL or medically eligible.
Benefits
Free diagnosis, treatment, antiretrovirals, counseling, disability aids, nutritional support.
Documents
Aadhaar, medical certificate, income proof (for BPL), disability ID (if applicable).
nhm.gov.in

F. Housing Schemes in India

1) Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY‑U)

Eligibility
Urban poor (EWS, LIG, MIG‑I & II), slum dwellers; first‑time homebuyer; no pucca house.
Benefits
Interest subsidy on home loans (up to ~₹2.67 lakh), financial aid for construction.
Documents
Aadhaar, income proof, property papers, bank details, self‑declaration of not owning a house.
pmaymis.gov.in

2) Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY‑G)

Eligibility
Rural poor households; name must appear in SECC 2011; priority to SC/ST, women‑headed households.
Benefits
₹1.2–₹1.3 lakh grant for house construction with basic amenities.
Documents
Aadhaar, SECC proof, bank account, MGNREGA job card (if any), caste certificate (if SC/ST).
pmayg.nic.in

3) MHADA Lottery Scheme (Maharashtra)

Eligibility
Resident of Maharashtra, 18+; income‑based categories (EWS/LIG/MIG/HIG).
Benefits
Affordable housing via lottery in cities like Mumbai, Pune, etc.
Documents
Aadhaar, PAN, income certificate, domicile proof, bank details.
mhada.gov.in

4) Slum Rehabilitation Scheme (SRA)

Eligibility
Slum dwellers (mainly Mumbai); residence prior to cut‑off date (as notified).
Benefits
Free pucca housing unit in same locality with better facilities.
Documents
Aadhaar, slum residence proof, biometric survey details, domicile certificate.
sra.gov.in

5) Affordable Rental Housing Complexes (ARHC)

Eligibility
Urban migrants, poor workers, students, laborers.
Benefits
Safe, affordable rental housing near employment zones.
Documents
Aadhaar, employment proof (if available), rental agreement.
pmaymis.gov.in

Project report, ID & residence proof, bank documents.
